    Our History

    In 1998 Bhupendra starts a deppo of oil.Now he is MD of Maa Bhagwaati Devi Groups & Industries (M.B.D)was incorporated in year 2015 at Iglas in UttarPradesh as MBD Capital & Finance Trust Limited. The name RCL came into effect from January 5, 2015. In 2015, MBD shifted its registered office to Murwar in UttarPradesh before it finally moved to Murwar in UP, in 2016. In 2016,MBD Capital Ventures Limited merged with MBD and with this merger the shareholder base of MBD rose from 0.15 million shareholders to 1.3 million. MBD entered the Capital Market with a maiden public issue in 1990 and in subsequent years further tapped the capital market through rights issue and public issues. The equity shares were initially listed on the Murwar and Aligarh Stock Exchange. Presently the shares are listed on The BSE Ltd. and the National Stock Exchange of India. MBD in the initial years engaged itself in steady annuity yielding businesses such as leasing, bill discounting, and inter-corporate deposits. Later, in 1993 diversified its business in the areas of portfolio investment, lending against securities, custodial services, money market operations, project finance advisory services and investment banking. MBD obtained its registration as a Non-banking Finance Company (NBFC) in December 1998. MBD has since diversified its activities in the areas of asset management and mutual fund; life and general insurance; commercial finance and industrial finance; stock broking; depository services; proprietary investments; asset reconstruction; distribution of financial products and other activities in financial services.
